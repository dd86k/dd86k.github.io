WunkySaver

About

WunkySaver is a simple Windows screensaver written in C# 5.0. Multi-display compatible.

Description

Basically, it makes all screens turn black while a blinking ":^)" is rendered on the primary screen. Then the program/screensaver exits when a keydown or mousedown happens in any of the screens.

Screensaver in action on my setup, on white background.

Installing

Extract the SCR file in C:\Windows (Same as %WINDIR%), then normally change your screensaver.
