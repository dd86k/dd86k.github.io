Twitter - AnimeSmugBot (@AnimeSmugBot)

Q: Why is the bot so slow sometimes?

This is due to how Twitter works.

The API will limit my searches (in this case: mentions) depending on 15 minutes blocks. It the limit gets reached, it blocks further requests for the rest of the 15 minutes block.

So, yes, it has to take "naps" often.
